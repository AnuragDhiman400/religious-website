About Sikh Gurus


There were ten recognised Sikh Gurus. In 1708, Tenth Guru,GURU GOBIND SINGH gave Guru Throne to final and eternal Guru, Guru Granth Sahib(Holy Book).

Sri Guru Granth Sahib is last and living Guru of the Sikhs.It is poetic compositions compiled by Guru Arjun dev ji Known as Adi Granth.It was First installed in Harmandir Sahib, Amritsar, India, on September 1, 1604.

GURU NANAK DEV (First Guru)


He was born at Nankana Sahib on 20 October , 1469 and founded Sikhism on 20 February , 1507. He remained Guru for 32 years and taught the folowers that "GOD IS ONE".

GURU ANGAD DEV (Second Guru)


He was born at Harike on 31 March , 1539 and became Guru on September 7, 1539.He introduced Gurmukhi Script(medium to write Punjabi language) in Khudur Sahib.

GURU AMAR DAS (Third Guru)


He was born at Basarke on 5 May , 1479 and became Guru on March 26, 1552 in Khadur.He started the Manji and Piri system to spread Sikhism.

GURU RAM DAS (Fourth Guru)


He was born at Chuna Mandi,Lahore on 24 September , 1574 and became Guru on September 1, 1574 .He was Guru for 7 years and organized the structure of Sikhism.

GURU ARJAN DEV (Fifth Guru)


He was born at Goindwal on 14 April , 1563 and became Guru on September 1, 1581. He lived as a Guru for 25 years and founded cities such as Tran Taran & Kartarpur.

GURU HAR GOBIND (Sixth Guru)


He was born at Guru ki Wadali(Amritsar) on 19 June , 1595 and became Guru on May 25,1606. During his era as Guru of 37 years, the Sikhs increased greatly in number.

GURU HAR RAI (Seventh Guru)


He was born at Kiratpur on 16 January , 1630 and became Guru on March 3,1644 in Kiratpur.He was a man of piece and established an Ayurvedic hospital.

GURU HAR KRISHAN (Eighth Guru)


He was born at Kiratpur on 7 July , 1656 and became Guru at the age of 5.He was also known as Bal Guru.He was having the shortest time as Guru of only two years.

GURU TEGH BAHADUR (Ninth Guru)


He was born at Amritsar on 1 April , 1621 and became Guru on August 11, 1664. He constructed many temples and contributed in many hymns to the Guru Granth Sahib.

GURU GOBIND SINGH (Tenth Guru)


He was born at Patna on 22 December , 1666 and became Guru on November 11, 1675.He created Khalsa on April 14, 1699, which was the major milestone of Sikhism.
