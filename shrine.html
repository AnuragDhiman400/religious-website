Golden Temple, Amritsar


Guru Arjan Dev ji originated an idea of making a worship place for Sikhs. Following this idea, he created the design of Sri Harminder Sahib.The site was bought by the Guru Ram Das Sahib.The Sikhs from all over the world pay obeisance at Sri Harminder Sahib.
